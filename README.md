## Totem Effects

Assistant to automatically farm totem effects

- Requires [**.NET 8 Desktop Runtime**](https://dotnet.microsoft.com/en-us/download/dotnet/8.0)

<img width="378" height="378" alt="screenshot" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c1dbbdaf-b1eb-469f-ace0-6544e2fe9dc8" />

> [!NOTE]
> Effects are blocked client-side to prevent lag, so they won't update visually in the Avatar Editor. After farming, re-enter the game to see your updated total in the Avatar Editor.
